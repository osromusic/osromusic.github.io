Sluberski Film Series

I was the director for the Sluberski Film Series at Concordia College, NY, from 2016-2021. We hosted public film screenings on campus of documentary films on important social issues. The goal was to start conversations between students and community members on important issues. Each screening was paired with an expert speaker or panel discussion followed by open questions. The series was founded by former professor and benefactor Dr. Thomas Sluberski and ran from 2015 until 2021 when the college ceased operations.
